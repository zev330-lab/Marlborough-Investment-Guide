Opportunity Set: On‑Market Deals by Strategy

This document consolidates the Balanced and Growth‑oriented screening results into a single “Opportunity Set.” Each section corresponds to a distinct investment strategy (analogous to a tab in a future application). Within each section, qualifying on‑market properties are listed with key underwriting assumptions, estimated financials and notes on risks or unknowns. Rent estimates are based on the moderate and aggressive figures compiled from MLS and external data sources[1][2]. Expense ratios and tax rates follow the deal‑selection logic defined earlier.

Conclusion

The Opportunity Set illustrates that relaxing cap‑rate floors (Balanced and Growth profiles) brings additional properties into consideration. 35 High St stands out as the only deal meeting both balanced and growth criteria. 111 Howe St offers creative upside through an ADU but carries greater execution risk. The remaining value‑add properties (102 Liberty St, 688 Boston Post Rd E, 30 Broad St) are suitable for growth‑oriented investors willing to accept lower initial yields for future rent increases and appreciation. All opportunities require further due diligence to confirm rents, expenses, renovation scope and regulatory compliance.
